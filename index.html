Curriculum Vitae

Hello! My name is Sarah Ronald and I am a regionally-based designer in Armidale NSW. With a background in art and design education, I have begun to develop my skills in full-stack development to fulfil my creative needs. Each new project presents its own individual challenges and I like to extend and learn new skills to solve these problems.

Recently earned a certificate in full stack development from the University of Sydney, advancing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js and Node.js. I completed the Coding Bootcamp course to develop my understanding of web design and development in particular. The ability to learn a new language and understand what it means fascinates me. I must admit, I thought my next language would be of a European flavour but current circumstances have pulled me in another direction.

My areas of strength include my ability to meet deadlines, communicate effectively, and collaborate with others. With each project my aim is to meet the need of the end-user, ensuring an interactive, aesthetic and functional interface. My ideal job would allow me to develop responsive websites and continue to teach these vital skills to others. Thanks for taking an interest in my portfolio. Enjoy!
